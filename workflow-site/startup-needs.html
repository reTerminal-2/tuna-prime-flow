2.4. Major Start-Up Needs

Facilities

Table No. 1: Facilities

Facility Type Description
Main Office Space A designated workspace where general business development, administrative tasks, and client consultations are conducted. It provides a professional environment for the team to manage day-to-day operations.
Internet & Storage Area A reliable area equipped with stable internet connectivity and secure storage for managing user accounts and business records. This area ensures smooth system operation and data safety.
Meeting & Training Area A shared space for internal team discussions, vendor training sessions, and presenting the platform to potential partners. It supports collaboration and professional engagement for all stakeholders.

Operational Equipment

Table No. 2: Operational Equipment

Equipment Description
Standard Laptops/Computers Used for general administrative work, managing the online shop inventory, and basic stock recording. These devices allow the owner to access the full administrative dashboard for strategic decision-making.
Smartphones/Mobile Devices Essential handheld devices used by staff and vendors to access the TunaFlow mobile interface. This ensures real-time sales monitoring and stock updates can be performed directly from the market stalls.
Router & Internet Access A stable, high-speed internet subscription and wireless router to maintain real-time synchronization between the local client devices and the TunaFlow cloud database (Supabase), ensuring all transactions and stock updates are processed without delay.
